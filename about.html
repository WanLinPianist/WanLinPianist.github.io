About

DMA in Piano Performance (UIUC, with Prof. Timothy Ehlen); MM (IU Jacobs, with Prof. André Watts). Dr. Lin performs internationally and curates lecture‑recitals on twentieth‑century Chinese piano repertoire.

Her recent programs include Wang Li‑San’s Ballade: Song of the Guerrillas, Chu Wang‑Hua’s The Red Star is Shining Brightly, and Du Ming‑Xin’s Piano Suite: The Red Detachment of Women, presented alongside canonical works to foster fresh listening.
